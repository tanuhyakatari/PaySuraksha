# PaySuraksha 🚀

### Because every day’s income matters.

---

## 🧠 Inspiration

India’s gig economy runs on millions of delivery partners who work daily under unpredictable conditions. A sudden downpour, extreme pollution, or unexpected curfew can instantly halt their work, cutting off their income for the day.

We identified a critical gap:
**While platforms depend on gig workers, workers themselves have no protection against income loss.**

PaySuraksha was created to ensure that even when work stops, income doesn’t.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Delivery partners (Swiggy, Zomato, Zepto, Amazon, etc.) lose **20–30% of their income** due to external disruptions such as:

* Heavy rainfall
* Floods
* High pollution
* Local restrictions or curfews

Currently, there is **no automated, real-time system** that compensates for these income losses.

---

## 💡 What It Does

**PaySuraksha** is a parametric insurance platform that:

* Monitors real-time environmental and social conditions
* Automatically detects disruptions
* Triggers claims without manual intervention
* Processes payouts instantly
* Prevents fraudulent claims using intelligent verification

👉 It acts as a **financial safety net for gig workers**

---

## 👤 Target Users

* Food delivery partners (Swiggy, Zomato)
* Grocery delivery workers (Zepto, Blinkit)
* E-commerce delivery agents (Amazon, Flipkart)

These users depend on **daily earnings**, making them highly vulnerable to disruptions.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works (Workflow)

1. User registers on the platform
2. System evaluates location-based risks
3. Weekly premium is assigned dynamically
4. Platform continuously monitors disruptions
5. Trigger conditions are met
6. Claim is automatically initiated
7. System verifies authenticity
8. Payout is processed instantly

---

## 💰 Weekly Pricing Model

| Risk Level  | Weekly Premium |
| ----------- | -------------- |
| Low Risk    | ₹20/week       |
| Medium Risk | ₹35/week       |
| High Risk   | ₹50/week       |

Premium is calculated based on:

* Environmental risk history
* Location patterns
* Activity behavior

---

## ⚡ Parametric Triggers

Claims are automatically triggered when:

* 🌧️ Rainfall exceeds threshold
* 🌫️ AQI crosses unsafe levels
* 🌊 Flood alerts are issued
* 🚧 Curfews or restrictions occur
* 📉 Platform outages happen

👉 No manual claim filing required

# 🔐 Adversarial Defense & Anti-Spoofing Strategy

🚨 **Challenge:** GPS spoofing attacks by coordinated fraud groups

---

## 1. Differentiation: Real vs Fake Users

PaySuraksha does not rely only on GPS.

Instead, it analyzes:

* Movement continuity
* Route patterns
* Activity behavior

👉 Real workers show natural patterns
👉 Fraudsters show unrealistic or static behavior

---

## 2. Multi-Layer Data Analysis

The system analyzes multiple signals:

* 📱 Device motion (accelerometer)
* 📊 App activity
* 🔋 Battery usage
* 📶 Network patterns
* 📍 Route consistency
* ⏱️ Active vs idle time

---

## 3. Fraud Ring Detection

* Identifies synchronized claim patterns across users
* Flags similar behavioral signatures
* Detects clusters in identical locations

👉 Enables detection of **organized fraud networks**

---

## 4. Trust-Based Verification System

| Trust Level | System Action        |
| ----------- | -------------------- |
| High        | Instant payout       |
| Medium      | Delayed verification |
| Low         | Manual review        |

👉 Ensures fairness while preventing fraud
